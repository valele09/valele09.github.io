Travel to Colombia!

Why you should visit Colombia?

Well, there are many different reasons, such as: the food, the landscapes, its culture, the places, the traditions, the people , etcetera etcetera.
Nevertheless, I will list some of those reasons down below!

Coffee

The distinguished coffee from this country comes from a rich history dating back to the 1730s. It has deep flavors of nuts and chocolate, along with floral and fruital overtones.

Biodiversity

Colombia is the second most biodiverse country in the world (behind Brazil), with a high variation on the species and its ecosystems.
Its flora and fauna its incredible, with over 56,000 species (where 9,000 of them are endemic), according to a study realized in 2016.

Landscapes

It is worldwide known that Colombia has beautiful sights, and with those come great landscapes and delightful places to visit and have a wonderful time.
A really known place is called The Lost City, which is found in the district of Santa Marta. This region in the north of the country is one of the Seven Wonders of Colombia.
